CHONKIE (aka pappy)

I first met this person in July 2020 when we were both quarantined little guys trying to make the best out of a shitty situation thanks to COVID-19. We found out that we had a lot of mutual interests in gaming and memes and often agreed on a lot of the same politics, if not practically all of it. To this day we remain in touch in one of my longest standing friendships, and I honestly couldn't imagine life without them. If you're a fan of Team Fortress 2, Guilty Gear, or even Deep Rock Galactic, then you'll probably see them around on these types of games. It isn't very often that I meet someone like Chonkie, but they're possibly one of the best friends I've ever had - it's genuinely hard to put the amount of care I have for them and their wellbeing into words since they did so much for me. They've been able to get me through my hardest endeavors, especially mentally, and I think I owe it to them to give as much as I can back. No sense in taking all good and never gifting any back! This website entry may not be much, but to me it means a lot to dedicate an entire entry to a person that has always been there for me. So, to Pappy, here's to another wonderful year and an even better friendship. Cheers, friend!

If you can, go subscribe to their YouTube channel and follow their Twitter. Why? Because I said so. <3
